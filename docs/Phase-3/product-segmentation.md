# Product Segmentation
Phase: 3
Status: Consolidated Draft

## 1. Target Customer Segments

Our market entry strategy targets three primary customer segments, each with distinct needs and willingness to pay. The product is segmented into a Good-Better-Best model to capture value across the market.

| Tier | Target Segment | Profile | Key Needs |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **SMBs & Startups** | Small to medium-sized businesses with limited budgets but a need for expert guidance. | Access to affordable expertise, process improvement, competitive analysis. |
| 2 | **Mid-Market Companies** | Established companies with dedicated departments and a need for more advanced analytics and integration. | Deeper insights, KPI tracking, integration with existing systems (e.g., QMS), full access to expert panels. |
| 3 | **Large Enterprises** | Large, complex organizations with significant budgets and a need for strategic oversight and custom solutions. | Strategic advisory, custom integrations, dedicated support, white-labeling, highest level of AI-driven oversight. |

## 2. Feature Segmentation by Tier

Features are allocated to tiers to create clear value differentiation and encourage upgrades.

| Feature | Starter (SMB) | Professional (Mid-Market) | Enterprise (Large) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **AI SME Access** | Basic (3 Experts) | Full Panel Access | Full Panel Access |
| **Assessments/Month** | 10 | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| **Reporting** | Standard Reports | KPI Dashboard | Custom Reporting & Dashboards |
| **QMS Integration** | ❌ | ✅ (Basic Connectors) | ✅ (Advanced & Custom Connectors) |
| **Persephone-AI Board** | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ |
| **Support** | Standard (Community & Email) | Priority (Dedicated Rep) | Dedicated Success Manager |
| **Customization** | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ (White-labeling, Custom Integrations) |

## 3. Key Projects & Product Alignment

| Key Project | Primary Target Tier(s) | Strategic Role |
|---|---|---|
| **QMS Integration** | Professional, Enterprise | The primary wedge for B2B enterprise acquisition, providing immediate, tangible value by optimizing existing quality processes. |
| **AI SME Enhancement** | All Tiers | The core IP and value driver, providing the expert insights that power the entire platform. The depth of access is a key differentiator between tiers. |
| **Persephone-AI Board** | Enterprise | The ultimate strategic differentiator, justifying the premium enterprise price point by offering virtual access to the world's leading AI minds for strategic oversight. |
| **Subscription System** | All Tiers | The foundational commercial layer that enables the entire tiered product strategy and manages recurring revenue. |
| **Customer Onboarding** | All Tiers | Ensures rapid time-to-value for all users, reducing churn and increasing the likelihood of upgrades. The level of personalization may vary by tier. |
