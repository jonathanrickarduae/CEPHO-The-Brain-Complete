# Phase 3: Market Entry - Feature List

## Subscription System

| Feature ID | Feature | Description |
|---|---|---|
| SUB-001 | Tiered Pricing Plans | Ability to create and manage multiple subscription tiers with different pricing and feature sets. |
| SUB-002 | Stripe Integration | Secure payment processing via Stripe for handling subscriptions, invoicing, and recurring payments. |
| SUB-003 | Subscription Management | Users can view their current plan, upgrade, downgrade, or cancel their subscription. |
| SUB-004 | Billing History | Users can view and download their past invoices. |
| SUB-005 | Free Trial | Offer a free trial period for new users to experience the platform before committing to a paid plan. |
| SUB-006 | Coupon and Discount Support | Ability to create and apply coupons and discounts to subscriptions. |

## Customer Onboarding

| Feature ID | Feature | Description |
|---|---|---|
| ONB-001 | Guided Setup Wizard | A step-by-step wizard to guide new users through the initial setup and configuration of their account. |
| ONB-002 | Personalization Questionnaire | A questionnaire to understand the user's role, industry, and goals to personalize their experience. |
| ONB-003 | Interactive Product Tour | An interactive tour to highlight key features and functionalities of the platform. |
| ONB-004 | Sample Data and Templates | Provide sample data and pre-built templates to help users get started quickly. |
| ONB-005 | Checklist and Progress Tracking | A checklist of tasks to complete during onboarding with progress tracking to motivate users. |
| ONB-006 | Welcome Email Series | A series of welcome emails to provide helpful tips, resources, and encouragement to new users. |
