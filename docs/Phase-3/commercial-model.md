# Commercial Models
Phase: 3
Status: Consolidated Draft

## 1. Pricing Strategy

The commercial model is built on a **three-tiered, feature-based subscription model**. This approach, validated by market research [4], allows the platform to serve a wide range of customer segments, from startups to large enterprises, while creating a clear path for customer upgrades.

| Tier | Name | Price Range (Monthly) | Target Segment | Value Proposition |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Starter** | $99 - $199 | SMBs, Startups | Access to affordable, AI-driven expert guidance for core business challenges. |
| 2 | **Professional** | $499 - $999 | Mid-Market | Deeper insights, full expert panel access, and integration with key business systems like QMS. |
| 3 | **Enterprise** | $2,500 - $10,000+ | Large Enterprises | Strategic advisory from the Persephone-AI board, custom solutions, and dedicated support for complex organizational optimization. |

## 2. Value Metrics

The primary value metric is the **depth of access to AI-driven expertise and the level of system integration**. As customers move up the tiers, they unlock:

-   Broader access to the AI SME Expert Panel.
-   Advanced features like the KPI Dashboard and QMS Integration.
-   Strategic oversight from the Persephone-AI Genius Board.

This feature-based gating provides a clear incentive for customers to upgrade as their needs become more sophisticated.

## 3. Payment & Billing Infrastructure

-   **Payment Gateway:** **Stripe** will be the exclusive payment processor. Its robust, PCI-compliant infrastructure [4] will handle all subscription billing, invoicing, and recurring payments.
-   **Subscription Management:** The platform will provide a self-service portal for users to manage their subscriptions, including upgrades, downgrades, cancellations, and viewing billing history.
-   **Free Trial:** A free trial period will be offered for the Starter and Professional tiers to drive product-led growth and allow users to experience the platform's value before committing to a paid plan.

## 4. Financial Projections (High-Level)

While a full financial model is a subsequent deliverable, the commercial strategy is based on the following financial considerations:

-   **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** The target blended CAC for the initial enterprise customers is **$4,000 - $6,000**, based on B2B SaaS benchmarks [5].
-   **Lifetime Value (LTV):** The pricing tiers are designed to ensure a healthy LTV-to-CAC ratio, with a target of 3:1 or higher.
-   **Revenue Streams:** The primary revenue stream will be recurring subscription fees. Future opportunities may include one-time deep-dive assessments or specialized consulting projects.
