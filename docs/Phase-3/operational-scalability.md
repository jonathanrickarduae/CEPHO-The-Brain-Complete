# Operational Scale
Phase: 3
Status: Consolidated Draft

## 1. Support Infrastructure

To support the initial cohort of paying customers, a multi-layered support infrastructure will be implemented.

| Support Channel | Description | Target Response Time |
|---|---|---|
| **Help Documentation Center** | A comprehensive, searchable knowledge base of help articles, tutorials, and FAQs. | Self-service, 24/7 |
| **Community Forum** | A community forum for users to ask questions, share best practices, and help each other. | Monitored by community managers. |
| **In-App Support Chat** | Real-time chat support for quick questions and troubleshooting. | < 5 minutes during business hours. |
| **Ticketing System** | A system to manage and track more complex user support requests. | < 24 hours for initial response. |

## 2. Customer Success

A dedicated customer success function will be established to ensure customers are achieving their desired outcomes.

-   **Onboarding Support:** Customer success managers will assist enterprise customers with the onboarding process.
-   **Proactive Outreach:** The customer success team will proactively reach out to users who may be at risk of churning.
-   **Quarterly Business Reviews (QBRs):** For enterprise customers, QBRs will be conducted to review progress, discuss goals, and identify new opportunities.

## 3. Technical Scalability

The platform is built on a scalable Phase 1/2 backend. However, the new integrations in Phase 3 will require careful monitoring and scaling.

-   **QMS Integration:** The QMS connectors will be designed to handle large volumes of data from enterprise systems. The data ingestion pipeline will be monitored for performance and scalability.
-   **Stripe Integration:** Stripe is a highly scalable platform that can handle a large volume of transactions.
-   **Database Scaling:** The database will be monitored for performance, and scaling strategies (e.g., read replicas, sharding) will be implemented as needed.

## 4. Team Scaling

The successful execution of Phase 3 will require hiring for key roles.

| Department | Key Roles to Hire |
|---|---|
| **Marketing** | Content Marketer, Community Manager |
| **Sales** | Account Executives, Sales Development Representatives (SDRs) |
| **Customer Success** | Customer Success Managers |

Recruitment for these roles should begin early in the 8-week launch plan to ensure the team is in place to support the first cohort of customers.
