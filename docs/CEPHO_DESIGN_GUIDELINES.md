# CEPHO.Ai: Official Design Guidelines

---

## 1. Introduction

This document provides official branding and design guidelines for all CEPHO.Ai reports, presentations, and internal documents. Its purpose is to ensure brand consistency, professionalism, and a unified visual identity across all communications. These guidelines are derived from the core design principles established in the CEPHO.Ai platform and the 100% Optimization Framework methodology.

Adherence to these standards is mandatory for all deliverables to maintain a cohesive and high quality brand image. CEPHO.Ai represents intelligent business management powered by AI, and all visual communications must reflect the sophistication, precision, and forward thinking nature of the platform.

---

## 2. Logo Usage

The official CEPHO.Ai logo must be placed at the top of the first page of every document. It should be clearly visible and not be altered, stretched, or recolored.

**Placement:** Top center or top left of the page.

**Exclusion Zone:** Maintain a clear space around the logo equivalent to the height of the "C" in the logo icon.

**Logo Variations:**
- Primary: Full color logo on white background
- Inverted: White logo on black background (for dark headers)
- Monochrome: Black logo for single color printing

## 4. Typography

Consistent typography is essential for readability and brand recognition. **Calibri** is the official font for all documents.

**Heading 1: Calibri, 24pt, Bold, Black**

This is the primary heading style for all major sections and document titles. It should be used sparingly to denote the most important divisions in the content. A thin black line should appear below H1 headings as a visual separator.

**Heading 2: Calibri, 18pt, Bold, Dark Grey**

This is the secondary heading style, used for sub sections within a major section. The dark grey color provides visual hierarchy while maintaining professional restraint.

**Heading 3: Calibri, 14pt, Bold, Black**

This is the tertiary heading style, used for smaller sub sections or to introduce specific points of interest. It maintains the primary brand color for consistency.

**Body Text:** All standard paragraph text should be Calibri, 11pt, in Black. Text should be left aligned. This ensures maximum readability for on screen and printed documents.

**Accent Text:** For highlighting key metrics, scores, or important callouts, use CEPHO Pink Neon (#ff006e) sparingly. This should only be used for numbers, percentages, or critical data points that require immediate attention.

## 5. Table Formatting

Tables are a key component of CEPHO.Ai reports. They must be clean, easy to read, and consistently styled.

**Header Row:** The background should be Light Grey (#F3F4F6) with Mid Grey (#666666) text, 10pt, Bold.

**Body Rows:** White background with Black (#000000) text, 10pt.

**Borders:** Use thin, light grey lines to separate cells.

**Alternating Rows:** For tables with more than 5 rows, use alternating white and very light grey (#FAFAFA) backgrounds for improved readability.

**Example Table:**

| KPI Category | Chief of Staff Score | SME Average | Status |
|--------------|---------------------|-------------|--------|
| Strategic Positioning | 45 | 48 | Needs Attention |
| Revenue Growth | 55 | 52 | Satisfactory |
| Customer Satisfaction | 72 | 75 | Good |
| Team Capability | 36 | 38 | Critical |

## 6. Chart Style

Charts and graphs should be visually clean and use the official brand colors to highlight data.

**Chart Types:** Use bar charts, line charts, and pie charts where appropriate. Heat maps are preferred for KPI scorecards.

**Colors:** Use CEPHO Black and CEPHO Pink Neon as the primary data colors. Use shades of grey for secondary data series.

**Labels:** All labels and axes should use Calibri, 10pt, in Black.

**Gridlines:** Should be thin and light grey to avoid visual clutter.

**Data Labels:** Key values should be displayed directly on charts in Black. Critical values (below 40) should be highlighted in CEPHO Pink Neon.

---

## 7. Page Layout

**Margins:** Use moderate margins (1 inch or 2.54 cm) on all sides.

**Page Numbers:** Do not include page numbers on CEPHO.Ai documents. This is a deliberate design choice to maintain clean, uncluttered pages.

**Headers:** Each page after the first should include a thin black line at the top with the document title in Calibri 10pt, left aligned.

**Footers:** Include "CEPHO.Ai | Confidential" in Calibri 9pt, Mid Grey, centered at the bottom of each page.

---

## 8. File Naming Convention

All final documents must follow a strict naming convention to ensure proper organization and version control.

**Format:** `CEPHO_Document_Name_DDMonYYYY_V#`

**Components:**
- **CEPHO_** : Brand prefix for all documents
- **Document_Name** : Descriptive title using underscores
- **DDMonYYYY** : Date of creation (e.g., 17Jan2026)
- **V#** : Version number (V1, V2, V3)

**Examples:**
- `CEPHO_KPI_HEATMAP_REPORT_17Jan2026_V1.pdf`
- `CEPHO_SME_ASSESSMENT_SUMMARY_17Jan2026_V1.pdf`
- `CEPHO_PROJECT_GENESIS_PLAYBOOK_17Jan2026_V2.pdf`

## 9. Report Structure Standards

All CEPHO.Ai reports should follow a consistent structure to ensure professionalism and ease of navigation.

**Standard Report Sections:**

1. **Cover Page:** Document title, date, version, CEPHO.Ai logo
2. **Executive Summary:** One paragraph overview of key findings and recommendations
3. **Methodology:** Brief description of how data was gathered and analyzed
4. **Findings:** Main body with data, analysis, and visualizations
5. **SME Expert Panel Scores:** Individual scores from each SME expert with names for follow up
6. **Recommendations:** Actionable next steps prioritized by impact
7. **Appendix:** Supporting data, detailed breakdowns, reference materials

**SME Score Presentation:**

When presenting SME expert scores, always include:
- Expert name and specialization
- Individual score for each category assessed
- Variance from average (highlight outliers)
- Contact reference for follow up discussions

## 10. Voice and Tone

All written content in CEPHO.Ai documents should maintain a professional consulting tone.

**Do:**
- Use clear, direct language
- Present data objectively before recommendations
- Provide actionable insights
- Reference sources and methodology
- Use complete sentences and paragraphs

**Do Not:**
- Use hyphens excessively (avoid AI generated appearance)
- Include gamification elements or casual language
- Make unsubstantiated claims
- Use bullet points as the primary content format
- Reference internal system names or AI agent identities

---

## 11. Quality Standards

All CEPHO.Ai documents must meet the following quality standards before distribution:

**Content Quality:**
- All data must be verified and sourced
- Recommendations must be supported by analysis
- No spelling or grammatical errors
- Consistent formatting throughout

**Visual Quality:**
- All charts and tables properly formatted
- Colors match brand palette exactly
- Logo placement correct
- Margins and spacing consistent

**Review Process:**
- Chief of Staff review required
- SME expert validation for technical content
- Quality score of 80/100 minimum before release
