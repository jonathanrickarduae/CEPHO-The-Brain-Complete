# Innovation Hub Workflows

This document explains the workflows and processes within the Innovation Hub, how they relate to the broader venture development framework, and how to use them effectively.

## Innovation Flywheel

The Innovation Hub is built around a 5-stage flywheel designed to capture, assess, and refine ideas:

1.  **Capture:** Idea intake from various sources, including:
    *   Manual idea submission
    *   Analyzing articles for opportunities
    *   Daily idea generation

2.  **Assess:** Strategic framework evaluation using a predefined set of questions covering:
    *   Market Analysis
    *   Feasibility
    *   Competitive Landscape
    *   Financial Viability
    *   Risk Assessment

3.  **Consult:** SME (Subject Matter Expert) feedback and validation.

4.  **Refine:** Iterate on the idea based on assessment and consultation findings.

5.  **Brief:** Generate an actionable summary and promote the idea to Project Genesis for further development.

## Venture Development Framework

The Innovation Flywheel is the first step in the broader 6-phase venture development framework:

*   **Phase 1: Ideation & Concept (Innovation Hub)**
*   **Phase 2: Planning & Strategy**
*   **Phase 3: Development & Prototyping**
*   **Phase 4: Testing & Validation**
*   **Phase 5: Launch & Commercialization**
*   **Phase 6: Scale & Optimization**

## How to Use the Innovation Hub

1.  **Capture Ideas:**
    *   Click **"Capture Idea"** to manually enter a new idea.
    *   Click **"Analyze Article"** to paste a URL and have the AI identify potential business opportunities.
    *   Click **"Generate Daily Ideas"** to have the AI generate new ideas based on current trends and your interests.

2.  **Assess Ideas:**
    *   Select an idea from the pipeline to view its details.
    *   Click **"Run Assessment"** to have the AI evaluate the idea against the strategic framework.

3.  **Consult with SMEs:**
    *   Click **"Consult with SMEs"** to get feedback from relevant experts.

4.  **Refine and Brief:**
    *   Based on the assessment and consultation, refine the idea and generate a brief.

5.  **Promote to Project Genesis:**
    *   Once an idea is validated and refined, it can be promoted to Project Genesis to begin the formal venture development process.
