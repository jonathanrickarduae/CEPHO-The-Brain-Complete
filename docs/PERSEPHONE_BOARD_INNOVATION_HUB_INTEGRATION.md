# Persephone Board: Innovation Hub Integration

## Overview
This document outlines the integration points for the Persephone Board (Expert Advisory) within the Innovation Hub process. The goal is to leverage expert input at critical stages to validate ideas, assess feasibility, and ensure strategic alignment.

---

## Integration Points

The Persephone Board will be engaged at two key stages of the Innovation Hub process:

1. **Assess Stage:** For initial validation and feasibility assessment.
2. **Brief Stage:** For final review and recommendation before promotion to Project Genesis.

---

## Assess Stage Integration

### Trigger
- An idea reaches the **Assess** stage in the Innovation Hub.
- The idea is flagged as high-potential or requires specialized knowledge.

### Process
1. **Expert Matching:** The Persephone Board Agent identifies and engages 1-3 relevant experts from the board based on the idea's category and required expertise.
2. **Briefing:** The experts receive a concise brief on the idea, including all available data and initial assessments.
3. **Review:** The experts provide their independent assessment of the idea's strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats (SWOT analysis).
4. **Feedback Consolidation:** The Persephone Board Agent consolidates all feedback into a single report and attaches it to the idea in the Innovation Hub.

### Outcome
- The idea is either validated and moves to the Refine stage, or it is archived with clear justification.
- The expert feedback provides valuable insights for refining the idea.

---

## Brief Stage Integration

### Trigger
- An idea has been fully refined and is ready for promotion to Project Genesis.
- A final go/no-go decision is needed.

### Process
1. **Final Review Panel:** The Persephone Board Agent assembles a final review panel of 3-5 experts, including at least one with commercialization expertise.
2. **Comprehensive Brief:** The panel receives a comprehensive brief, including the full business case, market analysis, and proposed project plan.
3. **Live Review Session:** A live (virtual) review session is held where the project champion presents the idea to the panel and answers questions.
4. **Final Recommendation:** The panel provides a final recommendation: **Go** (promote to Project Genesis), **No-Go** (archive), or **Revise** (send back for further refinement).

### Outcome
- A clear, data-driven decision is made on whether to invest in the idea and promote it to Project Genesis.
- The expert recommendations provide a strong foundation for the project's strategic direction.

---

This structured integration ensures that all ideas are rigorously vetted by experts before significant resources are committed, increasing the likelihood of success for new ventures.
