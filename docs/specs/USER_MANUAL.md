## 1. Welcome to CEPHO.AI

Welcome! This guide will walk you through the core features of CEPHO.AI and how to get the most out of your autonomous Chief of Staff.

---

## 2. Getting Started: The Onboarding Wizard

When you first log in, a guided wizard will help you set up your account.

1. **Connect Your Calendar:** Connect your Google Calendar to allow CEPHO.AI to understand your schedule.
2. **Connect Your Tools:** Connect Notion, Slack, and other tools to give your AI agents context.
3. **Set Your Preferences:** Tell CEPHO.AI when you want to receive your briefings and reviews.

---

## 3. Core Features

### 3.1. Victoria's Morning Briefing

Every morning, CEPHO.AI will deliver a personalized briefing to your dashboard.

- **What it is:** A concise summary of your day, including your top priorities, key insights from your AI agents, and your schedule.
- **How to use it:**
  1. Navigate to the **Victoria's Briefing** page from the sidebar.
  2. Read the summary and review your priorities.
  3. Click the **Download PDF** button to save a copy for offline reading.
  4. Click the **Listen to Audio** button to hear a podcast-style summary.

### 3.2. Project Genesis

Have a new idea? Use Project Genesis to turn it into a structured project.

- **What it is:** A guided wizard that helps you define and launch new strategic initiatives.
- **How to use it:**
  1. Navigate to the **Project Genesis** page.
  2. Click **New Project**.
  3. Follow the 7 steps to define your project's goals, phases, and requirements.
  4. Once complete, CEPHO.AI will automatically create the project, assign agents, and generate initial tasks.

### 3.3. AI Agents

Your team of specialist AI agents works for you 24/7.

- **What they are:** A team of 49 AI specialists in areas like finance, strategy, and marketing.
- **How to use them:**
  1. Navigate to the **AI Agents** page.
  2. View the registry of all available agents and their capabilities.
  3. Assign agents to your projects in Project Genesis.
  4. Agents will autonomously perform tasks, conduct research, and provide recommendations.

### 3.4. Evening Review

At the end of each day, CEPHO.AI helps you reflect on your progress.

- **What it is:** An automated summary of your day's accomplishments, challenges, and performance.
- **How to use it:**
  1. Navigate to the **Evening Review** page.
  2. Review the key wins and challenges from the day.
  3. Add your own reflections and set priorities for tomorrow.

## 4. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: How does CEPHO.AI learn?**

A: Your AI agents learn in two ways: by researching their specialist field daily, and by receiving feedback from you on the quality of their work. The more you use the platform, the smarter it gets.

**Q: Is my data secure?**

A: Yes. All your data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard security practices. See our Security Policy for more details.

**Q: Can I connect other tools?**

A: Yes. Navigate to **Settings > Integrations** to connect a wide range of tools like Notion, Slack, Trello, and more.
