# Persephone Board: Project Genesis Integration

## Overview

This document outlines the integration points for the Persephone Board (Expert Advisory) within the Project Genesis process. The goal is to provide strategic guidance, risk oversight, and commercialization support for all new ventures.

---

## Integration Points

The Persephone Board will be engaged at three key stages of the Project Genesis process:

1. **Strategic Forming Phase:** For initial strategy validation and roadmap development.
2. **Mid-Project Review:** For course correction and risk mitigation.
3. **Final Review & Commercialization:** For go-to-market strategy and final approval.

---

## Strategic Forming Phase Integration

### Trigger

- A new project is created in Project Genesis.
- The initial project plan and roadmap are being developed.

### Process

1. **Strategic Review Panel:** The Persephone Board Agent assembles a strategic review panel of 3-5 experts with relevant industry and market expertise.
2. **Roadmap Review:** The panel reviews the project's strategic goals, target market, competitive landscape, and proposed roadmap.
3. **Workshop Session:** A workshop is held with the project team to challenge assumptions, identify potential blind spots, and refine the strategic direction.

### Outcome

- A robust, validated strategic plan and roadmap for the project.
- Early identification of potential market and technology risks.

---

## Mid-Project Review Integration

### Trigger

- The project reaches a major milestone (e.g., completion of MVP).
- A significant change in the project's scope, budget, or timeline occurs.

### Process

1. **Progress Audit:** The Persephone Board Agent engages 1-2 experts to conduct a quick audit of the project's progress against its goals.
2. **Risk Assessment:** The experts assess any new or emerging risks and provide recommendations for mitigation.
3. **Course Correction:** If needed, a session is held with the project team to discuss any necessary course corrections.

### Outcome

- The project stays on track and aligned with its strategic objectives.
- Proactive risk management and mitigation.

---

## Final Review & Commercialization Integration

### Trigger

- The project is nearing completion and preparing for market launch.
- A final decision is needed on the go-to-market strategy and commercialization plan.

### Process

1. **Commercialization Panel:** The Persephone Board Agent assembles a commercialization panel with expertise in marketing, sales, and business development.
2. **Go-to-Market Review:** The panel reviews the proposed go-to-market strategy, pricing, and sales channels.
3. **Final Approval:** The panel provides a final recommendation on whether the project is ready for market launch and approves the commercialization plan.

### Outcome

- A well-defined, expert-validated go-to-market strategy.
- Increased likelihood of successful market adoption and commercial success.

---

This multi-stage integration ensures that all projects benefit from expert guidance throughout their lifecycle, from initial strategy to final market launch.
