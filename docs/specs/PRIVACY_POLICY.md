# Privacy Policy

_Last Updated: 2026-03-01_

This Privacy Policy explains how CEPHO.AI ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information about you. This policy applies when you use our website, services, and mobile applications (collectively, the "Services").

## 1. Information We Collect

- **Account Information:** Name, email address, password.
- **User Content:** Data you provide when you use our Services, such as project details, tasks, and documents.
- **Integration Data:** Data from third-party services you connect, such as Google Calendar or Notion.
- **Usage Information:** Log data, IP address, browser type, and device information.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- To provide, maintain, and improve our Services.
- To personalize your experience.
- To communicate with you.
- For security and fraud prevention.

## 3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

- **Service Providers:** Third parties who perform services on our behalf (e.g., Supabase, OpenAI, AWS).
- **Legal Requirements:** If required by law or to protect our rights.

## 4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Under GDPR, you have the right to data portability and to object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@cepho.ai.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you our Services. We will delete your information upon request, subject to legal and operational requirements.
