# CEPHO Platform Features

This document provides a comprehensive overview of the key features and capabilities of the CEPHO platform, covering all 57 pages and their functionalities.

---

## 1. Core Dashboard & Briefings

### The Nexus Dashboard (`/`)

The Nexus is the central command center of the CEPHO platform, providing a real-time overview of your operations.

- **Key Metrics:** Displays 4 key stat cards: Expert Consultations, Documents, Projects, and Insights.
- **System Performance:** Visualizes system health with indicators for accuracy, response time, satisfaction, and uptime.
- **Expert Rankings:** Shows the top 5 performing AI experts with their consultation counts.
- **Activity Timeline:** A real-time feed of recent activities and events.

### Daily Brief & The Signal (`/daily-brief`)

This flagship feature provides a personalized morning briefing from Victoria Sterling, your virtual presenter.

- **AI-Generated Briefing:** A 2-3 minute summary of your day with audio playback.
- **Export Options:** Export the briefing as a PDF, video, or podcast.
- **5-Tab Interface:** Includes Overview, Schedule, Intelligence, Strategy, and Action Engine.
- **Decision Workflow:** Action buttons for each item: Got it, Defer, Delegate, and Assign to Chief of Staff.

### Morning Signal (`/morning-signal`)

A focused morning dashboard to help you start your day.

- **Stat Cards:** 4 cards showing Tasks Ready, Needs Attention, Insights, and Overnight Work.
- **Task Prioritization:** A list of high-priority tasks for the day.
- **Quick Actions:** Buttons for View Schedule, Open Workflow, AI Experts, and Digital Twin.

## 2. AI & Experts

### Persephone Board (`/persephone`)

A virtual board of 14 top AI industry leaders providing strategic oversight.

- **14 AI Leaders:** Featuring Sam Altman, Jensen Huang, Dario Amodei, and more.
- **Impact Scores:** Each leader has an impact score and specialization area.
- **Meeting Schedule:** View upcoming board meetings and agendas.
- **Decision Tracking:** A history of recent board decisions and voting results.

### AI Experts & SMEs (`/ai-experts`)

A comprehensive database of 311 expert specialists.

- **Filtering & Search:** Filter experts by panel (Blue Team, Red Team, etc.) and category (AI SMEs, Corporate Roles, etc.).
- **Expert Profiles:** Each expert has a detailed profile with performance score, project count, and specialty.
- **Team Assembly:** Assemble custom teams of experts for specific projects.

### Digital Twin & Chief of Staff (`/digital-twin`)

An AI executive assistant that learns from you and works alongside you.

- **Chat Interface:** A conversational interface for interacting with your digital twin.
- **Task Management:** View and manage tasks assigned to your Chief of Staff.
- **Training & Quality Gates:** Monitor the training progress and performance of your digital twin.
