2. Market Position

2.1 Target Customer Who is your primary customer?
First Pass
Solo founders and small business CEOs (1-10 employees) who lack access to executive support but need strategic guidance across multiple business functions. They are time poor, knowledge constrained, and need a trusted advisor who understands their context.
2.2 Competitive Differentiation What makes CEPHO.Ai unique?
First Pass
Unlike generic AI assistants, CEPHO.Ai provides a complete AI executive team: Virtual Chief of Staff (coordination), Victoria Head of Briefings (communication), and AI SME Expert Panel (functional expertise). The system learns your preferences, maintains context across all interactions, and delivers expert level guidance with quality gated by Jim Short (100/100 Top Trumps standard).
2.3 Definition of Success What does "number one" mean? (Not just revenue)
First Pass
Being number one means maximizing mental bandwidth liberation for users. Success is measured by how much cognitive load we remove from entrepreneurs, allowing them to focus on what they actually want to do: building their business. Revenue follows when we genuinely free people's brains.
