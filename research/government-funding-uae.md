# UAE Government Funding Programs Research

## 1. Mohammed Bin Rashid Innovation Fund (MBRIF)

### Programs Available

#### A. Accelerator Program
- **Type**: Non-financial support (mentorship, services)
- **Equity**: Zero-fee policy, no dilution of equity
- **Benefits**:
  - Tailored services rooted in collaboration
  - World-class mentorship focused on your needs
  - Access to global team of experts
  - No fees, no equity dilution

#### B. Guarantee Scheme
- **Type**: Government-backed credit guarantee
- **Equity**: No dilution - you keep 100% ownership
- **Benefits**:
  - Flexible financing to enable growth
  - Government-backed guarantee reduces risk
  - Access to financing from partner banks
  - Application process: ~10 business weeks

### Eligibility Criteria (Guarantee Scheme)

**Priority Sectors**:
- Technology
- Education
- Renewable Energy
- Water
- Transport
- Health
- Space

**Requirements**:
- UAE Presence: Must be based in UAE
- Growth Stage: Proven track record, viable for market expansion
- Revenue: Already generating and growing revenue
- Market Potential: Innovation with large impact potential

### Application Process
1. **Stage One - Application**: Register and submit online
2. **Stage Two - Light Due Diligence**: Eligibility and preliminary clearance
3. **Stage Three - Heavy Due Diligence**: Innovation, commercial, industry, credit risk analysis
4. **Stage Four - Committee**: Advisory & Decision Committee review
5. **Stage Five - Decision**: Letter of Intent (LOI) if approved, then lender assessment

**Timeline**: Maximum 10 business weeks (if application complete)

## 2. Khalifa Fund for Enterprise Development

### Funding Products

#### A. Microfinance Loan
- **Target**: New entrepreneurs
- **Project Cost**: Up to specified limit
- **Purpose**: Starting small businesses

#### B. SME Funding
- **Target**: Established SMEs
- **Support**: Business funding and growth support

### Membership Programme
- For non-funded SMEs or members not needing funding
- Business support services available

### 2024 Performance
- 650+ deals facilitated
- Worth AED 672 million

### Required Documents
- Valid passport copy
- Valid Emirates ID
- Family Book
- Security clearance form
- Salary certificate (if applicable)
