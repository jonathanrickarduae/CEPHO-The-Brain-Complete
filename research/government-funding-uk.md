# UK Government Funding Programs Research

## 1. R&D Tax Credits

### SME R&D Tax Relief (Pre-April 2024)
- **Eligibility**: Accounting periods starting before 1 April 2024
- **Benefit**: 186% total deduction (100% normal + 86% extra)
- **Payable Credit**: 10-14.5% of surrenderable loss

### SME Criteria
- Less than 500 staff
- Turnover under €100 million OR balance sheet under €86 million
- Must include linked/partner companies in calculations

### Merged R&D Scheme (Post-April 2024)
- For accounting periods beginning on or after 1 April 2024
- Combines SME and RDEC schemes
- Enhanced R&D Intensive Support (ERIS) available

### Intensity Condition (for higher rate)
- R&D expenditure must be at least 40% of total expenditure
- Qualifies for 14.5% payable credit rate

### Qualifying R&D Activities
- Scientific or technological advancement
- Resolution of scientific/technological uncertainty
- NOT arts, humanities, or social sciences

### Excluded Projects
- Arts, humanities, social sciences (including economics)
- Projects with >€7.5m in R&D/vaccine relief
- Subcontracted work
- Subsidized projects (grants)

## 2. Innovate UK Smart Grants

### Overview
- Competitive funding for innovative projects
- UK-registered companies only
- Projects must be carried out in UK

### Project Requirements
- **19-24 month projects**: £100,000 - £1 million total costs
- Must be collaborative for larger projects
- Demonstrate innovation impact

### Eligibility
- UK-registered SME (for solo projects)
- UK-registered business of any size (for collaborative)
- Research and technology organizations can participate

### Application Process
- Highly competitive
- Must demonstrate:
  - Innovation impact
  - Ability to deliver
  - Commercial potential
  - Technical feasibility

## 3. British Business Bank Start Up Loans

### Loan Details
- **Amount**: Up to £25,000 per person (max £100,000 per business)
- **Type**: Unsecured personal loan
- **Interest**: Fixed rate
- **Repayment**: 1-5 years

### Eligibility Criteria
- 18 years or older
- UK resident
- Starting new business OR trading up to 36 months
- Unable to secure finance elsewhere (self-declaration OK)
- Business based in UK
- Right to work in UK
- Pass credit checks
- Can afford repayments

### Required Documents
- Business Plan
- Cash Flow Forecast
- Personal Survival Budget

### Additional Benefits
- 12 months free mentoring
- Business support resources

### Excluded Business Types
- Weapons
- Chemical manufacture
- Pornography
- Drugs/illegal activities
- Banking/money transfer (FCA regulated)
- Gambling/betting
- Property investment
- Commission-only agents
- Charities (CICs may be considered)

### Excluded Loan Purposes
- Debt repayment
- Training/education programs
- Non-business investments

### Business Partnerships
- Multiple partners can apply (up to £25,000 each)
- Maximum £100,000 per business total
- Each partner assessed separately
- Must have equity stake and controlling interest
- 50% share required for applicants

## 4. Enterprise Investment Scheme (EIS)

### Overview
- Tax relief for investors in qualifying companies
- Encourages investment in early-stage businesses

### Investor Benefits
- 30% income tax relief on investments up to £1m/year
- Capital gains tax exemption on EIS shares
- Loss relief available

### Company Requirements
- Less than £15m gross assets
- Fewer than 250 employees
- Trading for less than 7 years
- Qualifying trade

---

## 5. Seed Enterprise Investment Scheme (SEIS)

### Overview
- Higher tax relief for very early-stage companies
- Designed for seed/startup funding

### Investor Benefits
- 50% income tax relief on investments up to £200,000/year
- Capital gains tax exemption
- Loss relief available

### Company Requirements
- Less than £350,000 gross assets
- Fewer than 25 employees
- Trading for less than 3 years
- Qualifying trade

---

## 6. Patent Box

### Overview
- Reduced Corporation Tax rate on profits from patented inventions
- 10% effective tax rate (vs standard 25%)

### Eligibility
- UK company subject to Corporation Tax
- Own or exclusively license qualifying patents
- Patents granted by UK IPO, EPO, or certain EEA states
