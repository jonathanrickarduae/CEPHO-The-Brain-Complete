To attract sophisticated biotech investors, provide more rigorous detail on clinical development strategy, intellectual property, and scientific basis for R&D efforts."

### Maria Venture (Venture Capital)
> "A compelling, large-scale vision with a strong strategic partner. However, aggressive financial projections and excessive length will likely create skepticism. Ground the ambitious vision in a more focused, phased, and verifiable execution plan."

### Jennifer CFO (Financial Expert)
> "A compelling, high-growth opportunity with strong strategic pillars. However, financial projections and valuation claims are aggressive and require substantial evidence. Focus on de-risking the financial case by providing detailed assumptions, clear capital allocation, and robust valuation support."

### Nova Storyweaver (Storytelling)
> "A strong foundation with a clear investment thesis and compelling market opportunity. However, the sheer volume of information dilutes the emotional impact. Condense, humanize, and create a sense of inevitability to make the pitch truly memorable."

### Philip Strategy (Strategic Planning)
> "A strong strategic foundation with a compelling first-mover narrative and a credible partner in Celadon Pharmaceuticals. However, the presentation's length and complexity obscure the core value proposition. Streamline the narrative and strengthen the competitive moat articulation."

### Simon Brand (Brand Strategy)
> "The deck has a solid foundation with a well-defined color palette and a clear brand concept. However, the visual execution needs to be more consistent and impactful. Focus on creating a unified visual language that reinforces the brand story at every touchpoint."

### Fatima Al-Hassan (GCC Investment)
> "The deck presents a compelling opportunity aligned with UAE's Vision 2031 diversification goals. However, it underestimates the importance of demonstrating deep regional understanding and cultural alignment. Strengthen the regional narrative and provide concrete evidence of government relationships."

### Rachel Regulatory (Regulatory Affairs)
> "The deck presents a compelling first-mover opportunity but underestimates the complexity of the regulatory pathway. Strengthen the regulatory narrative with specific timelines, contingency planning, and evidence of proactive regulator engagement to build investor confidence."
