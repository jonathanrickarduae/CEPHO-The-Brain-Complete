# Digital Twin Comprehensive Learning Assessment

## 200 Questions to Accelerate Your Digital Twin's Understanding

### Instructions

This assessment builds a complete profile of how you think, decide, communicate, and operate. Your Digital Twin will use this to mirror your thinking patterns and advise accordingly.

**How to Answer:**
- **Type A (100 questions)**: Scale 1-10 (1 = Strongly Disagree/Never, 10 = Strongly Agree/Always)
  - Format: A1-7, A2-9, A3-5...
- **Type B (100 questions)**: Yes/No
  - Format: B1-Y, B2-N, B3-Y...

**Time Required:** 60-90 minutes
**Tip:** Answer instinctively - first response is most accurate.
