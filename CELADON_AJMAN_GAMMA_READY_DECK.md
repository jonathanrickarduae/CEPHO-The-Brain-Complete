## APPENDIX D: The Science of the Endocannabinoid System (Slides 34-35)

### Slide 34: How Cannabinoids Work

**The Endocannabinoid System (ECS)**
A biological system present in all mammals, responsible for maintaining homeostasis across multiple physiological processes.

**Key Components:**
| Component | Function |
|-----------|----------|
| CB1 Receptors | Primarily in brain and nervous system; regulate pain, mood, appetite |
| CB2 Receptors | Primarily in immune system; regulate inflammation and immune response |
| Endocannabinoids | Body's natural cannabinoids (anandamide, 2-AG) |
| Enzymes | Break down endocannabinoids after use |

### Slide 35: Therapeutic Applications

**Validated Medical Applications:**

| Condition | Evidence Level | Mechanism |
|-----------|----------------|-----------|
| Chronic Pain | Strong | CB1/CB2 modulation reduces pain signaling |
| Epilepsy | Strong | Anticonvulsant properties (FDA-approved Epidiolex) |
| Multiple Sclerosis | Moderate | Reduces spasticity and pain |
| Chemotherapy-Induced Nausea | Strong | Antiemetic effects via CB1 |
| Anxiety/PTSD | Emerging | Modulates stress response |
