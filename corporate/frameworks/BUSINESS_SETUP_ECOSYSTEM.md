# Business Setup Ecosystem

**Version:** 1.0  
**Created:** 18 January 2026  
**Purpose:** Define the complete process for setting up a new business through CEPHO.Ai

---

## Overview

The Business Setup Ecosystem is the comprehensive framework that guides entrepreneurs and organizations through the process of establishing a new business. It integrates all CEPHO.Ai capabilities to provide end-to-end support from initial concept to operational launch.

---

## The Business Setup Journey

### Stage 1: Ideation & Validation

| Step | Tool/Process | Output |
|------|--------------|--------|
| 1.1 | Project Genesis Wizard | Business concept captured |
| 1.2 | Market Opportunity Assessment | TAM/SAM/SOM analysis |
| 1.3 | Competitive Landscape Review | Competitor matrix |
| 1.4 | SME Panel Consultation | Multi-perspective validation |

### Stage 2: Strategic Foundation

| Step | Tool/Process | Output |
|------|--------------|--------|
| 2.1 | **Founder Story Generator** | Business-centric origin narrative |
| 2.2 | Strategic Framework Questionnaire | Vision, mission, values defined |
| 2.3 | One-Page Strategy | Strategic summary document |
| 2.4 | Business Model Canvas | Revenue and value model |

### Stage 3: Planning & Documentation

| Step | Tool/Process | Output |
|------|--------------|--------|
| 3.1 | Business Plan Generator | Comprehensive business plan |
| 3.2 | Financial Projections | 3-5 year financial model |
| 3.3 | Pitch Deck Creator | Investor presentation |
| 3.4 | Executive Summary | One-page investment overview |

### Stage 4: Corporate Structure

| Step | Tool/Process | Output |
|------|--------------|--------|
| 4.1 | Legal Entity Selection | Recommended structure |
| 4.2 | Governance Framework | Board and management structure |
| 4.3 | Compliance Checklist | Regulatory requirements |
| 4.4 | Risk Assessment | Risk register and mitigation |

### Stage 5: Operational Readiness

| Step | Tool/Process | Output |
|------|--------------|--------|
| 5.1 | Technology Stack Selection | Recommended tools and platforms |
| 5.2 | Process Design | Core operational workflows |
| 5.3 | Team Structure | Organizational chart |
| 5.4 | Launch Checklist | Go-live preparation |

## Founder Story Integration

The Founder Story Generator is a critical component of Stage 2 (Strategic Foundation). It creates the emotional narrative that:

- Connects with investors and stakeholders
- Provides consistent messaging across all materials
- Establishes the "why" behind the business
- Differentiates from competitors

### Integration Points

| Document | How Founder Story Integrates |
|----------|------------------------------|
| Pitch Deck | "Our Story" slide content |
| Business Plan | Executive Summary opening |
| Website | "About Us" page narrative |
| Press Releases | Background paragraph |
| Investor Meetings | Opening narrative |

## Project Genesis Wizard Integration

The Project Genesis Wizard captures the initial inputs needed for the entire ecosystem:

### Required Inputs for Founder Story

| Input | Captured In | Used For |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| Industry/Sector | Genesis Step 1 | Problem context |
| Target Customer | Genesis Step 2 | Audience definition |
| Core Problem | Genesis Step 3 | Pain point articulation |
| Solution Category | Genesis Step 4 | Solution framing |
| Key Differentiator | Genesis Step 5 | Unique positioning |

### Optional Inputs (Enhance Authenticity)

| Input | Captured In | Used For |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| Founder Industry Experience | Genesis Step 6 | Credibility building |
| Observation Moment | Genesis Step 6 | "Aha" narrative |
| Motivation Type | Genesis Step 6 | Emotional driver |
| Geographic Context | Genesis Step 1 | Regional relevance |

## SME Panel Involvement

Each stage of the Business Setup Ecosystem leverages specific SME expertise:

| Stage | Primary SMEs | Secondary SMEs |
|-------|--------------|----------------|
| Ideation & Validation | Strategy, Marketing | Finance, Operations |
| Strategic Foundation | Strategy, Marketing, Psychology | All |
| Planning & Documentation | Finance, Strategy, Legal | All |
| Corporate Structure | Legal, Finance, Risk | Strategy |
| Operational Readiness | Operations, Technology, People | All |

---

## Quality Gates

Each stage must pass quality review before proceeding:

| Gate | Criteria | Reviewer |
|------|----------|----------|
| G1: Concept Validated | Market opportunity confirmed, competitive differentiation clear | Strategy SME |
| G2: Foundation Complete | Story compelling, strategy coherent, model viable | Full SME Panel |
| G3: Documentation Ready | All documents complete, consistent, investor-ready | Jim Short QA |
| G4: Structure Approved | Legal and governance appropriate for stage | Legal SME |
| G5: Launch Ready | All systems operational, team in place | Operations SME |
