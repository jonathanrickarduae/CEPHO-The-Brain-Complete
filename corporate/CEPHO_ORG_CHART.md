# CEPHO.Ai

## Organizational Structure

---

### Executive Summary

CEPHO.Ai operates with a unique organizational model that combines human leadership with AI powered executive functions. This structure enables a single CEO to operate with the capability of a full executive team while maintaining strategic oversight through virtual advisory boards.

## Role Definitions

### CEO (Human)
The human leader who sets vision, makes final decisions, and focuses on building the business. CEPHO.Ai exists to free the CEO's mental bandwidth by handling everything else.

### Persephone-AI: The AI Genius Board
Virtual NED and Steering Committee composed of 14 digital advisors based on the world's most influential AI leaders. Provides strategic oversight on infrastructure, design, technology, and growth accountability.

**The 14 Members:**
1. Sam Altman (OpenAI) - AGI Development, Consumer AI
2. Jensen Huang (Nvidia) - AI Chips, Hardware Infrastructure
3. Jony Ive (io/OpenAI) - AI Device Design, Human-AI Interface
4. Mark Zuckerberg (Meta) - Social AI, Superintelligence
5. Tekedra Mawakana (Waymo) - Autonomous Vehicles, Physical AI
6. Palmer Luckey (Anduril) - AI Weapons, Defence Technology
7. CC Wei (TSMC) - Chip Manufacturing, Supply Chain
8. Liang Wenfeng (DeepSeek) - Open Source AI, China AI Strategy
9. Elon Musk (Tesla/xAI) - Robotics, Brain-AI Interface
10. Fei-Fei Li (Stanford/World Labs) - AI Ethics, Human-Centered AI
11. Demis Hassabis (Google DeepMind) - AI for Science, Drug Discovery
12. Satya Nadella (Microsoft) - Enterprise AI, Cloud Infrastructure
13. David Sacks (US Government) - AI Policy, Regulation
14. Mira Murati (Thinking Machines Lab) - AI Research, Model Development

### Virtual Chief of Staff
The AI executive that does the grunt work. Manages daily operations, coordinates the SME Expert Panel, learns user preferences, and handles tasks autonomously where possible.

### Victoria: Head of Briefings
AI role responsible for delivering all briefings to the CEO. Presents Morning Signal, evening reviews, and polished outputs from the Chief of Staff. Voice enabled with ElevenLabs integration.

### AI SME Expert Panel
Eight domain experts providing specialized knowledge:
- Finance SME
- Legal SME
- Strategy SME
- Operations SME
- Marketing SME
- People SME
- Technology SME
- Risk SME

### Advisory Network
External data sources and integrations that feed intelligence into the system. Includes market data, competitive intelligence, industry news, and third party APIs.

### AI Infrastructure
Backend systems powering the platform including LLM integration, voice synthesis, document generation, and data processing.

## Quality Assurance

**Jim Short: Ultimate Quality Gatekeeper**
100/100 Top Trumps rating. All outputs pass through Jim Short review before delivery to CEO. If it passes Jim, it's ready for the real world.

---

## Interaction Model

1. **CEO Request** → Virtual Chief of Staff receives and interprets
2. **Task Assignment** → Chief of Staff assigns to relevant SME experts
3. **Strategic Check** → Persephone-AI consulted for major decisions
4. **Work Execution** → SME Panel produces deliverables
5. **Quality Review** → Jim Short validates output quality
6. **Briefing Delivery** → Victoria presents polished results to CEO
7. **Learning Loop** → Chief of Staff learns from CEO feedback
