# Persephone-AI

## The AI Genius Board

---

### Executive Summary

Persephone-AI is CEPHO.Ai's virtual Non-Executive Director (NED) Board and Steering Committee, composed of digital representations of the 14 most influential people shaping the global AI landscape. This board provides strategic guidance on infrastructure, design, technology decisions, and business growth accountability.

The name Persephone references the Greek goddess who bridges two worlds, symbolizing the connection between human genius and artificial intelligence.

---

## Purpose

Persephone-AI serves as the intellectual foundation for CEPHO.Ai's strategic direction by:

1. **Strategic Guidance** - Informing infrastructure, design, and technology decisions
2. **Growth Accountability** - Holding the business accountable for its next stage of growth
3. **Knowledge Repository** - Maintaining a deep dive repository of insights from the world's leading AI minds
4. **Future Forecasting** - Understanding where AI is heading based on those driving it forward

## Integration with CEPHO.Ai

Persephone-AI integrates into the CEPHO.Ai ecosystem as follows:

### Organizational Position
- Reports directly to the CEO alongside the Virtual Chief of Staff
- Operates as a strategic advisory layer above the AI SME Expert Panel
- Provides oversight and direction for major technology and business decisions

### Decision Framework
When facing strategic decisions, the Chief of Staff can consult Persephone-AI to understand:
- What would Sam Altman do? (Consumer product strategy)
- What would Jensen Huang do? (Infrastructure decisions)
- What would Demis Hassabis do? (Research direction)
- What would Satya Nadella do? (Enterprise partnerships)

### Knowledge Application
The deep dive repository feeds into:
- Morning Signal briefings on AI industry developments
- Strategic planning and roadmap decisions
- Technology stack and infrastructure choices
- Business model and commercialization strategy

## Phase 3 Implementation

Persephone-AI is a Phase 3 (Market Entry) project with the following milestones:

### Milestone 1: Repository Foundation
- Compile comprehensive source list for each member
- Structure database schema for knowledge capture
- Build initial scraping and ingestion pipeline

### Milestone 2: Knowledge Extraction
- Process books, articles, and transcripts
- Extract key insights, predictions, and frameworks
- Tag and categorize by topic and relevance

### Milestone 3: Digital Advisor Creation
- Create AI personas for each member
- Train on extracted knowledge base
- Develop query interface for strategic consultation

### Milestone 4: Integration
- Connect to Chief of Staff decision workflows
- Build Morning Signal integration for AI industry updates
- Create accountability dashboard for growth metrics
