## 1. Brand Overview

### 1.1 Brand Identity

CEPHO.AI represents the convergence of human intelligence and artificial intelligence. The name derives from the Greek word for "brain" (κεφάλι), symbolising where intelligence begins. Our brand embodies sophistication, precision, and the seamless integration of AI into executive decision making.

### 1.2 Brand Promise

We deliver intelligent augmentation for business leaders, providing a Chief of Staff powered by AI that thinks, learns, and acts with the precision and discretion expected at the highest levels of business.

### 1.3 Brand Values

| Value | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Intelligence | Every output demonstrates deep thinking and strategic insight |
| Precision | Accuracy and attention to detail in all communications |
| Discretion | Professional restraint and appropriate confidentiality |
| Evolution | Continuous learning and improvement |
| Trust | Reliability and consistency in all interactions |

## 2. Logo Usage

### 2.1 Primary Logo

The CEPHO.AI logo consists of the animated brain icon with neural connections alongside the wordmark "CEPHO" in uppercase letters.

### 2.2 Logo Placement

| Document Type | Logo Position | Size |
|---------------|---------------|------|
| Reports & Briefs | Top left corner | 40px height |
| Presentations | Title slide: centered; Content slides: top left | 60px / 30px |
| Email Communications | Header left | 30px height |
| Internal Documents | Top left corner | 35px height |

### 2.3 Clear Space

Maintain minimum clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "C" in CEPHO on all sides.

### 2.4 Logo Variations

| Variation | Usage |
|-----------|-------|
| Full colour (cyan/magenta gradient) | Primary use on dark backgrounds |
| Monochrome white | Dark backgrounds where colour is not appropriate |
| Monochrome black | Light backgrounds, print documents |
| Monochrome grey | Watermarks, subtle branding |

### 2.5 Logo Restrictions

The following are not permitted:
- Stretching or distorting the logo
- Changing logo colours outside approved palette
- Placing logo on busy or low contrast backgrounds
- Adding effects such as shadows or outlines
- Rotating the logo

## 4. Typography

### 4.1 Primary Typefaces

| Purpose | Font | Weight | Size Range |
|---------|------|--------|------------|
| Headings | Inter | Bold (700) | 24px - 48px |
| Subheadings | Inter | Semibold (600) | 18px - 24px |
| Body Text | Inter | Regular (400) | 11pt - 12pt |
| Captions | Inter | Regular (400) | 9pt - 10pt |
| Data/Tables | Inter | Regular (400) | 10pt - 11pt |

### 4.2 Document Typography

For formal documents and reports:

| Element | Font | Size | Colour |
|---------|------|------|--------|
| Document Title | Inter Bold | 24pt | Black |
| Section Heading | Inter Semibold | 16pt | Black |
| Subsection | Inter Semibold | 13pt | Dark Grey |
| Body Paragraph | Inter Regular | 11pt | Black |
| Table Header | Inter Semibold | 10pt | Black |
| Table Body | Inter Regular | 10pt | Dark Grey |
| Page Numbers | Calibri Regular | 10pt | Light Grey |
| Footer Text | Inter Regular | 9pt | Medium Grey |

### 4.3 Typography Rules

- Avoid hyphens in compound words to maintain natural readability
- Use sentence case for body text
- Use title case for headings and section titles
- Maintain consistent line height of 1.5 for body text
- Paragraph spacing: 12pt after paragraphs

## 5. Document Templates

### 5.1 Executive Summary Template (2 Pages)

**Page 1 Layout:**
- Logo: Top left, 40px height
- Document title: Below logo, 24pt Inter Bold
- Date and classification: Right aligned, 10pt grey
- Executive summary section: 2-3 paragraphs
- Key findings table: 3-5 bullet points in table format

**Page 2 Layout:**
- Recommendations section
- Next steps
- Appendix reference
- Chief of Staff sign-off block

### 5.2 Full Report Template

**Cover Page:**
- Centred logo (60px)
- Report title
- Subtitle/description
- Date
- Classification
- Prepared by: CEPHO.AI Chief of Staff

**Content Pages:**
- Header: Logo (30px) left, document title right
- Footer: Page number right (Calibri 10pt light grey)
- Margins: 2.5cm all sides
- Two column layout for data heavy sections

**Appendix Pages:**
- Numbered A1, A2, etc.
- Scoring matrices in table format
- Supporting data and calculations

### 5.3 Innovation Brief Template

**Structure:**
1. Executive Summary (1 page)
2. Opportunity Overview (1 page)
3. Strategic Assessment Summary (1 page with scoring matrix)
4. Expert Panel Recommendations (1 page)
5. Investment Scenarios (1 page)
6. Appendices (detailed scoring, expert viewpoints)

## 7. Quality Assurance Process

### 7.1 Chief of Staff Sign-Off

All documents generated by CEPHO.AI must pass through the Chief of Staff quality assurance layer before delivery.

**QA Checklist:**

| Check | Requirement |
|-------|-------------|
| Brand Compliance | Logo placement, colours, typography correct |
| Content Quality | Clear, concise, professional language |
| Accuracy | Data and calculations verified |
| Completeness | All required sections present |
| Formatting | Consistent with template standards |
| Classification | Appropriate confidentiality marking |

### 7.2 Sign-Off Block

Every document must include the following sign-off block:

```
---
Document Quality Assurance

Prepared by: CEPHO.AI
Reviewed by: Chief of Staff
Date: [DD Month YYYY]
Status: [Draft / Final]
Classification: [Internal / Confidential / Restricted]

This document has been reviewed for accuracy, completeness, 
and compliance with CEPHO.AI brand and quality standards.
---
```

## 8. Writing Style

### 8.1 Tone of Voice

CEPHO.AI communications should be:
- Professional and authoritative
- Clear and direct
- Confident but not arrogant
- Practical and actionable
- Thoughtful and considered

### 8.2 Language Guidelines

| Do | Avoid |
|----|-------|
| Use active voice | Passive constructions |
| Be specific and concrete | Vague generalisations |
| Use plain English | Jargon and buzzwords |
| Present balanced analysis | Dramatic or sensational language |
| Provide actionable recommendations | Abstract observations |

### 8.3 Prohibited Terms

Avoid the following terms that can appear overly dramatic or AI-generated:
- "Paradox"
- "Crisis" (unless genuinely applicable)
- "Revolutionary"
- "Game-changing"
- "Synergy"
- Excessive use of hyphens in compound words

## 9. Document Classification

### 9.1 Classification Levels

| Level | Description | Handling |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| Public | Suitable for external distribution | No restrictions |
| Internal | For CEPHO.AI users only | Do not share externally |
| Confidential | Sensitive business information | Need to know basis |
| Restricted | Highly sensitive | Named recipients only |

### 9.2 Classification Marking

Classification must appear:
- On the cover page (if applicable)
- In the document header or footer
- In the sign-off block

## 10. Implementation

### 10.1 Scope

These guidelines apply to all documents generated by CEPHO.AI including:
- Innovation Flywheel outputs
- Project Genesis documents
- Daily Briefs and Signals
- Evening Review summaries
- Expert consultations
- Strategic assessments
- Investment analyses

### 10.2 Compliance

All document generation processes must:
1. Reference these guidelines
2. Use approved templates
3. Pass Chief of Staff QA review
4. Include proper sign-off block

### 10.3 Updates

These guidelines are maintained by the Chief of Staff and reviewed quarterly. Proposed changes should be submitted for review and approval.
