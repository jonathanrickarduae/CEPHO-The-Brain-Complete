# Chief of Staff Training Program

**Document ID:** TRAIN-001  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** January 15, 2026  
**Purpose:** Comprehensive training curriculum for the Chief of Staff role within CEPHO

---

## Executive Summary

The Chief of Staff serves as the critical quality gate between AI-generated outputs and final decisions. This training program provides a structured curriculum to develop the competencies required to excel in this role, ensuring consistent quality across all CEPHO operations.

**Training Duration:** 40 hours (8 modules × 5 hours each)  
**Delivery Format:** Self-paced with practical exercises  
**Certification:** Competency assessment after each module

---

## Part 1: Role Overview

### 1.1 Chief of Staff Mission

> "To ensure every decision is informed by comprehensive analysis, validated by expert perspectives, and aligned with strategic objectives."

### 1.2 Core Responsibilities

| Responsibility | Description | Time Allocation |
|----------------|-------------|-----------------|
| Quality Assurance | Review and validate all AI-SME outputs | 30% |
| Process Oversight | Ensure workflows follow established processes | 20% |
| Gap Identification | Identify missing analysis and request additional research | 15% |
| Stakeholder Coordination | Bridge between AI team and human stakeholders | 15% |
| Continuous Improvement | Capture learnings and update processes | 10% |
| Documentation | Maintain records and audit trails | 10% |

### 1.3 Success Metrics

| Metric | Target | Measurement |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Output Quality Score | >90% | Stakeholder satisfaction ratings |
| Process Compliance | 100% | Audit checklist completion |
| Turnaround Time | <24 hours | Time from draft to validated output |
| Gap Detection Rate | >95% | Issues caught before stakeholder review |
| Learning Capture | 100% | All engagements documented |

## Part 2: Training Curriculum

### Module 1: CEPHO System Foundations (5 hours)

**Objective:** Understand the complete CEPHO ecosystem and how all components integrate.

**Topics:**
1. CEPHO Vision and Mission
2. The Brain Platform Architecture
3. QMS Framework Overview
4. Document Hierarchy and Naming Conventions
5. Integration Points (CRM, Gamma, ElevenLabs)

**Required Reading:**
- CEPHO Vision and Gap Analysis
- QMS Architecture Document
- System Architecture Blueprint

**Practical Exercise:**
- Navigate The Brain platform and identify all major components
- Map the document hierarchy from memory

**Competency Assessment:**
- [ ] Can explain CEPHO's purpose in one sentence
- [ ] Can identify all 4 QMS layers
- [ ] Can navigate The Brain platform independently
- [ ] Understands document naming conventions

## Part 3: Certification Process

### 3.1 Assessment Structure

| Component | Weight | Pass Threshold |
|-----------|--------|----------------|
| Module Quizzes (8) | 40% | 80% each |
| Practical Exercises | 30% | Complete all |
| Final Case Study | 20% | 85% |
| Oral Examination | 10% | Pass/Fail |

### 3.2 Final Case Study

The candidate will receive a new opportunity brief and must:

1. Complete intake assessment (30 minutes)
2. Review AI-generated Deep Dive (45 minutes)
3. Validate Business Plan (45 minutes)
4. Prepare executive summary (30 minutes)
5. Present to panel (15 minutes)
6. Answer questions (15 minutes)

**Total Time:** 3 hours

### 3.3 Certification Levels

| Level | Requirements | Privileges |
|-------|--------------|------------|
| **Associate** | Complete Modules 1-4 | Can assist with reviews |
| **Certified** | Complete all modules + final case | Full validation authority |
| **Senior** | 6 months experience + 10 engagements | Can train others |
| **Principal** | 1 year experience + process improvements | Strategic oversight |
