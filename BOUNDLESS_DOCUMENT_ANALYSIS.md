## Iqbal Meeting Briefing Structure

**Objective:** Intelligence gathering — no commitments. Understand capabilities, expectations, vision.

**14 Discussion Topics Across 6 Categories:**
1. Commercial & Capabilities (3 topics): Scaling, Enterprise Readiness, Network Relationships
2. Operations & Delivery (2 topics): Day-to-Day Operations, Service & Support
3. Equity & Governance (3 topics): Valuation, Governance, Vesting
4. UAE & Global Operations (3 topics): UAE Plans, Global Perspective, Claims Validation
5. App Development & Strategic Vision (3 topics): IP Ownership, Value View, Exit Alignment

**Key Advice:** Be careful about making money directly at this stage. Don't commit commercially before structure is right.
