# CEPHO.Ai Brand and Style Guidelines

**Document Type:** Brand Guidelines  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** 18 January 2026  
**Classification:** Internal Reference

---

## 1. Introduction

This document establishes the visual identity and document formatting standards for CEPHO.Ai. These guidelines ensure brand consistency and professionalism across all reports, presentations, and platform interfaces.

**Brand Name:** CEPHO - From the Greek word for brain (kephali). Where intelligence begins.

**Scope:** All commercial, technical, and internal documents produced for CEPHO.Ai.

**Mandatory Adherence:** All documents and interfaces representing CEPHO.Ai must follow these guidelines.

---

## 2. Logo Usage

### Placement

The CEPHO logo (animated brain visualization) should appear at the top of the first page of all documents.

**Placement Options:**
- Top center (preferred for cover pages)
- Top left (preferred for internal documents)

### Exclusion Zone

Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "C" in CEPHO. This ensures the logo remains visually distinct.

### Logo Integrity

- Do not alter the logo proportions
- Do not stretch or compress the logo
- Do not recolor the logo outside approved colors
- Do not place the logo on busy backgrounds
- The animated brain glow effect uses cyan/teal

## 4. Typography

### Font Family

**Primary Font:** Calibri (documents), Inter (platform interface)

Calibri is used for all formal documents. Inter is used for the web platform interface.

### Document Heading Hierarchy

**Heading 1**
- Font: Calibri
- Size: 24pt
- Weight: Bold
- Color: Black (#000000) or White (#FAFAFA on dark)
- Usage: Major sections and document titles

**Heading 2**
- Font: Calibri
- Size: 18pt
- Weight: Bold
- Color: Pink (#E91E8C)
- Usage: Sub-sections within major sections

**Heading 3**
- Font: Calibri
- Size: 14pt
- Weight: Bold
- Color: Black (#000000) or White (#FAFAFA on dark)
- Usage: Smaller sub-sections

**Body Text**
- Font: Calibri
- Size: 11pt
- Weight: Regular
- Color: Black (#000000) or White (#FAFAFA on dark)
- Alignment: Left aligned

### Platform Typography

## 5. Table Formatting

### Header Row

- Background: Dark Grey (#1A1A1A) or Light Grey (#F3F4F6 on light)
- Text Color: White (#FAFAFA) or Grey (#666666 on light)
- Font Size: 10pt
- Font Weight: Bold

### Body Rows

- Background: Black (#0D0D0D) or White (#FFFFFF on light)
- Text Color: White (#FAFAFA) or Black (#000000 on light)
- Font Size: 10pt
- Font Weight: Regular

### Borders

- Style: Thin solid lines
- Color: Border Grey (#333333)
- Application: All cell borders

---

## 6. Document Formatting

### Page Layout

- Margins: 1 inch (2.54 cm) on all sides
- No page numbers (user preference)
- No hyphens in compound words

### Section Dividers

- Use horizontal rules (---) to separate major sections
- Maintain consistent spacing before and after dividers

### File Naming Convention

**Format:** `CEPHO_Document_Name_DDMonYYYY`

**Examples:**
- `CEPHO_KPI_SCORECARD_18Jan2026.pdf`
- `CEPHO_BRAND_GUIDELINES_18Jan2026.pdf`

## 7. Platform Components

### Buttons

**Primary Button**
- Background: Pink (#E91E8C)
- Text: White (#FAFAFA)
- Hover: Darker Pink (#C4177A)
- Border radius: 6px
- Padding: 8px 16px

**Secondary Button**
- Background: Transparent
- Border: 1px solid Border Grey (#333333)
- Text: White (#FAFAFA)
- Hover: Accent Grey background

**Ghost Button**
- Background: Transparent
- Text: Muted (#A3A3A3)
- Hover: Accent Grey background

### Cards

- Background: Card Dark (#1A1A1A)
- Border: 1px solid Border Grey (#333333)
- Border radius: 8px
- Padding: 24px
- Shadow on hover

### Inputs

- Background: Background Black (#0D0D0D)
- Border: 1px solid Border Grey (#333333)
- Border radius: 6px
- Padding: 8px 12px
- Focus: Pink ring (2px)
- Placeholder: Muted text

## 9. Icons and Imagery

### Icon Style

- Library: Lucide React
- Sizes: 16px (sm), 20px (md), 24px (lg)
- Color: Inherit from text color
- Stroke width: 2px

### Brain Logo

- Animated glowing brain visualization
- Glow color: Cyan/Teal
- Used in header and onboarding

### Avatar Style

- Shape: Circular with border
- Fallback: Initials on colored background
- Sizes: 32px (sm), 40px (md), 48px (lg)

## 10. Motion and Animation

### Transition Durations

| Name | Duration | Usage |
|------|----------|-------|
| fast | 150ms | Hover states |
| normal | 200ms | Most transitions |
| slow | 300ms | Page transitions |

### Easing Functions

- Default: ease-in-out
- Enter: ease-out
- Exit: ease-in

### Animation Guidelines

- Respect prefers-reduced-motion
- Keep animations subtle and purposeful
- No distracting or playful animations
- Loading states: Pulse or shimmer

---

## 11. Accessibility

### Color Contrast

- Text on background: Minimum 4.5:1 ratio
- Large text: Minimum 3:1 ratio
- Interactive elements: Clear visual distinction

### Focus States

- Visible focus ring on all interactive elements
- Ring color: Primary Pink (#E91E8C)
- Ring width: 2px
- Ring offset: 2px

### Keyboard Navigation

- All interactive elements focusable
- Logical tab order
- Escape to close modals
- Arrow keys for navigation

## 12. Brand Voice

### Key Attributes

- **Professional:** Slick, sophisticated, enterprise-ready
- **Intelligent:** AI-powered, data-driven, insightful
- **Efficient:** Time-saving, productivity-focused
- **Trustworthy:** Secure, reliable, transparent

### Design Principles

1. Clarity over complexity - Every element serves a purpose
2. Dark-first design - Optimized for extended use
3. Pink neon accents - Distinctive brand color for emphasis
4. No gamification - Professional interface without childish elements
5. Voice-first consideration - Designed for hands-free interaction

### Writing Style

- Professional and authoritative
- Clear and concise
- Confident without being aggressive
- No emojis in formal documents
