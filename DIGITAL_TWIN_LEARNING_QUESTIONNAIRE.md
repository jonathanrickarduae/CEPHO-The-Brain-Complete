# Digital Twin Learning Questionnaire

## Instructions

This questionnaire accelerates your Digital Twin's understanding of how you think, decide, and operate. Complete all 100 questions to build a comprehensive profile.

**How to Answer:**
- **Type A (Scale 1-10)**: Answer with the question number and score, e.g., "A1: 7" or "A1-7"
- **Type B (Yes/No)**: Answer with the question number and response, e.g., "B1: Yes" or "B1-Y"

**Time Required:** 45-60 minutes
**Tip:** Answer instinctively - your first response is usually most accurate.
