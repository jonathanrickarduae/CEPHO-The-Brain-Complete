# Cepho UI/UX Review - SME Team Guide

## Overview

Thank you for reviewing the Cepho application's user interface and experience. Your feedback is critical in ensuring the platform meets professional standards and provides an intuitive, consistent experience across all pages and features.

This guide explains the review process, what to look for, and how to provide structured feedback.

---

## Review Objectives

The Cepho UI/UX review has three primary goals:

1. **Design Consistency** - Ensure all pages follow the Manus design language (colors, typography, spacing, components)
2. **Usability & Clarity** - Verify that navigation is intuitive, information hierarchy is clear, and interactions are obvious
3. **Accessibility & Performance** - Confirm that the app is accessible to all users and performs smoothly

## What to Look For

### Design Language Compliance
- Are colors consistent with the Manus palette (primary, secondary, foreground, muted-foreground)?
- Is typography using the correct sizes and weights?
- Are spacing and padding consistent (multiples of 4px, 8px, 12px, 16px)?
- Are borders and shadows subtle and consistent?

### Usability
- Can you easily find what you're looking for?
- Are buttons and interactive elements clearly labeled?
- Do hover/focus states provide clear feedback?
- Is the information hierarchy logical?
- Can you complete key tasks without confusion?

### Accessibility
- Can you navigate using only the keyboard?
- Are focus states clearly visible?
- Is text readable (sufficient contrast)?
- Are images and icons properly labeled?

### Responsiveness
- Does the layout adapt well to mobile (< 768px)?
- Are touch targets at least 44x44px on mobile?
- Is there any horizontal scrolling on mobile?
- Does the desktop layout have appropriate max-width?

---

## Common Issues to Watch For

### Color & Contrast
- White text on light backgrounds (hard to read)
- Low contrast ratios (should be 4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text)
- Hard-coded colors instead of semantic tokens
- Inconsistent color usage across pages

### Typography
- Inconsistent heading sizes
- Text too small to read comfortably
- Line height too tight (should be 1.5-1.75)
- Font weights not matching design system

### Spacing
- Inconsistent padding inside components
- Uneven margins between sections
- Too much or too little whitespace
- Elements feeling cramped or scattered

### Navigation
- Active state not clearly indicated
- Menu items hard to find
- Inconsistent navigation across pages
- Mobile menu not working properly

### Components
- Button styles not consistent
- Form inputs missing labels or focus states
- Modal overlays not semi-transparent
- Cards not visually distinct from background

## Severity Levels for Issues

When reporting issues, classify them by severity:

| Severity | Definition | Examples |
|----------|-----------|----------|
| **Critical** | Breaks functionality or accessibility | Text unreadable, button doesn't work, keyboard navigation broken |
| **High** | Significantly impacts user experience | Inconsistent colors, confusing navigation, poor mobile layout |
| **Medium** | Noticeable but doesn't block usage | Minor spacing issue, inconsistent hover state, missing icon |
| **Low** | Minor polish issue | Slightly off alignment, could use better animation, typo |

## Questions to Ask Yourself

As you review, consider these questions:

1. **First Impression** - Does the app feel professional and polished?
2. **Navigation** - Can I easily find what I'm looking for?
3. **Clarity** - Is the purpose of each page immediately clear?
4. **Consistency** - Do all pages follow the same design language?
5. **Accessibility** - Can I use this without a mouse? Is text readable?
6. **Mobile** - Does this work well on a phone?
7. **Performance** - Does everything load quickly and respond smoothly?
8. **Feedback** - Do I know when something is loading, saving, or has failed?
